Procore: Account Setup, Login and App Installation



Procore is widely used Construction Project management platform. To begin, log in to Procore account, enter Email & Password. This allows Project owners, contractors and project collaborators to form central collaboration hub to complete Project.


Initiate your Project management, enhance quality, transfer data autoimatically, choose Projects transfer and Field safety with Procore Login.

How to gain access to Procore? Simply, setup your Procore account on any devices - Laptop, mobile devices and on web.

  1. Visit to Procore Sign Up page.
  2. Now, put in following details- Name & Email Id.
  3. Put in details of your company.
  4. Tap 'Search' button.
  5. Next to your company's name, click 'Request to Join'.
  6. Choose your role from drop down list.
  7. Now, if you didn't find any company then, tap 'Create Account'.

How do I log into Procore for the first time?


For logging into your Procore account,


  1. Visit login.procore.com website.
  2. Now, put in your Email Id & Password.
  3. Then, tap on the 'Log In' button.

Get into Procore Web Account by simply entering your Login credentials. Alternatively, you may receive 'Welcome to Procore Invite' link, you can join Procore website as User.







Join using Welcome to Procore Invite Email Invitation

On trying to login your Procore account, you'll receive Welcome To Procore Link:


  • First of all, open your Welcome to Procore Invite link.
  • Then, tap on the 'Click Here to Create Your New Password'. Now, create password page opens.
  • Now, put in Procore web login password in the field.
  • Further, put in the Password in Confirm Password field.
  • Mark the Check box and tap on 'Set Password'.
  • Nextly, check the 'I Agree to the Terms and Conditions' box.
  • Lastly, click on the 'Agree' to login your Procore Account.

Install an App

For installing an App from Procore, you need to grant 'Admin' from Compay directory tools.

  1. Navigate to Procore official website.
  2. Now, perform Procore login.
  3. Then, tap on the App tile, you need to download.
  4. Thereafter, tap on the 'Install App' button.
  5. Lastly, follow the on-screen instructions for app installation.
